Our Story

Started in Portland,
built for Portland

We started Ridgeline Junk Removal with one truck and a simple belief: junk removal shouldn't be complicated or overpriced. Portland homeowners deserve a reliable, friendly service they can actually trust.

We're not a franchise. We're a local team that shows up on time, works hard, and leaves your space cleaner than we found it. Every load we haul is a neighbor we've helped — and that means something to us.

We also take recycling and responsible disposal seriously. Whenever possible, we donate usable items to local charities and recycle materials rather than sending everything to the landfill.
